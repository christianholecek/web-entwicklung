Participant Information & Consent

Thank you very much for participating!
Please read the following information carefully before you begin.

What you will do: You will read short sentences aloud and record yourself. The session takes approximately 20 minutes. You will complete two tasks — you may choose the order.

Before you start (important):

  • Please use a laptop or desktop computer (not a phone or tablet).
  • Find a quiet room — close windows and silence notifications.
  • Sit about 30–50 cm from your microphone and speak clearly at a normal volume.
  • For each recording: press Start recording, wait one second, speak, wait one second, then press Stop.

Microphone permission: Your browser will ask for microphone access — please click Allow. You can make a test recording before starting. Test recordings are not saved.

Saving recordings: After each sentence click Accept & save. Your recording will upload — this may take a few seconds. Once the upload is complete you can move to the next sentence.

Data: Audio recordings will be stored securely and used only for research purposes. Data will be anonymised for publication.

Voluntary participation: Participation is voluntary. You may withdraw at any time before submission.

Contact: If you have questions or encounter technical problems, please contact the researcher who sent you this link.
